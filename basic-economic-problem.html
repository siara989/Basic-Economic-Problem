Basic Economics Problem

What is actullay the basic economic proplem?

It is actually the scarcity in other words

Planet earth's resource capacity is limited and the human needs and wants increse day by day.

This mismatch between limited resources and unlimted wants creates a problem called scarcity.

Producers attempt to satisfy the consumers by producing products using the limited resources

but they are faced with other economic problrms faced by evry nations in this world.

  • What to produce?
  • How to produce?
  • For whome to produce?
  • In what quantity?
